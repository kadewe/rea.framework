PackageMainApplication
Classpublic class MainAppManager
InheritanceMainAppManager flash.events.EventDispatcher

The core controller class. Manages the loading and unloading of the modules as well as the communication between them and the model related data. Modules a loaded by name, their functions are accessed via ICustomModule interface. The view component of the modules get connected with the DisplayManager
